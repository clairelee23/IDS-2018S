Where should international students seek jobs?

People nuturally care about their future lives. For international students who want to improve their careers in the United States, H-1B visa is an important problem. Thus, the data of 2017 employer requests for H-1B nonimmigrant workers are analyzed to give insights for international students into where to seek jobs.

Thanks to the Office of Foreign Labor Certification (OFLC), who offers the public data of H-1B visas.

The status of H1-B visa requests associated with the last significant event or decision (2017)

1.4% requests were denied last year, which means at least one in a hundred requests is likely to be denied. This is a problem which international students are worried about.
